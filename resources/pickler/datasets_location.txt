This textfile contains the information related to the source files from
which Elim got the data sets in `resouces/pickled_data/`. Here, Elim
only cares about selection variables at Level 4 and higher (i.e. Level 3
variables are ignored).

#### GRECO
#### 
#### 1. Pickled files in `greco/preweighted/` are obtained
####    from Michael's pickled files. These files are baseline
####	only and do not have the info needed for re-weighting.
#### 2. To pickle L4 and L5 variables from scratch, use level 5
####    i3 files.
#### 3. Pickled files in `greco/final/` are obtained from the
#### 	Level 7 pickled files. These files include systematic
####	sets and has everything needed for re-weighting.
#################################################################

#### DRAGON
####
#### 1. Lower level datasets in DRAGON are made afterwards
####    because i3Files at earlier levels by MSU group have
####	a BDT > 0.1 cut. Those files cannot be used to make
####	selection plots at lower selection levels.
#### 2. Because files are made afterwards, the newer files
#### 	have no L4 / 5 cuts applied.
#### 3. No 'preweighted' files were created. But, Elim
####    includes pre-calculated weights when pickling.
